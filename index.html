The Atelier

A movement built
by one pair of hands.

I.
Raw Material Selection

18-carat gold alloys and surgical-grade steel are assessed individually. Only material with verified provenance enters the workshop.

II.
Movement Assembly

137 individual components are assembled under 40x magnification. Each bearing surface is polished to a mirror finish by hand.

III.
Casing & Regulation

Once cased, the watch is worn on a wrist machine for 21 days before final regulation to ±1 second daily.

IV.
Provenance Certificate

Each piece ships with the name of its maker, recorded hours, and a numbered certificate of authenticity.
